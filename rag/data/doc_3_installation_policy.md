# Commercial Client Installation and Return Policy

## 1. Professional Installation Policy
- **Authorized Installation**: All commercial grade lighting installations (recessed office panels, industrial high bays, and solar street lights) must be supervised or executed by a certified electrician or an authorized AL ROUF Installation Partner.
- **Pre-Installation Site Audit**: A site audit is mandatory prior to the installation of solar street lighting to ensure proper pole positioning, optimal sun exposure, and lack of nearby shade/obstruction.
- **Safety Standard Compliance**: All wiring and fixture mounting must comply strictly with local electrical codes and AL ROUF safety manuals. Unauthorized modifications to LED drivers or solar charge controllers void the warranty immediately.

## 2. Commercial Return & Exchange Policy
- **Return Window**: Commercial clients are entitled to request returns or exchanges for unused, undamaged, and standard fixtures in original packaging within **30 days** of delivery.
- **Restocking Fee**: Standard catalog returns are subject to a **10% restocking fee** to cover warehouse logging and quality inspections.
- **Custom Lighting & RFQs**: Custom-designed lighting solutions, custom powder-coated fixtures, or volume quotes filled through direct factory RFQs are **non-refundable** and cannot be returned unless confirmed defective upon arrival.
- **Defective Products**: Defective products covered under warranty will be repaired or replaced by AL ROUF at no additional cost. Return requests for defective items must be filed via the Commercial Client Portal with accompanying site logs and photographic evidence.
