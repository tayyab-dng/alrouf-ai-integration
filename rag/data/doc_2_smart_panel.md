# Smart Office LED Panel

## Product Specifications

- **Product Name**: Smart Office LED Panel
- **Model Number**: AR-SP-6060-IOT
- **Dimensions**: 600mm x 600mm x 10mm (fits standard recessed ceiling grids)
- **Wattage**: 40W
- **Luminous Efficiency**: 110 lm/W (Total 4,400 lumens)
- **Input Voltage**: AC 220-240V, 50/60Hz
- **Color Temperature (CCT)**: Tunable White from 2700K (Warm White) to 6500K (Cool Daylight)
- **Color Rendering Index (CRI)**: Ra > 90 (high color accuracy)
- **Dimmability**: Smooth dimming from 1% to 100% via smart controls
- **IoT & App Compatibility**: 
  - Compatible with the 'AL ROUF Smart Link' mobile application (iOS & Android)
  - Seamless integration with Google Assistant, Amazon Alexa, and Apple HomeKit
  - Supports standard Zigbee 3.0 and Wi-Fi (2.4 GHz) communication protocols
  - Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) enabled for easy local commissioning and onboarding
- **Warranty**: 3 Years Standard Warranty (covers driver and LED panel assembly)

## Overview & Smart Automation
The Smart Office LED Panel is designed to create optimal working environments through automated circadian rhythm scheduling. Users can customize color temperature and brightness dynamically based on the time of day, helping boost productivity and energy savings.
